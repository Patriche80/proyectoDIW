I am Paco Hidalgo, a self-taught artist and painter with a great passion for art and the Fallas of Valencia. My path as an artist began in my youth, when I discovered my love for painting and started experimenting with different techniques and styles. As my skill and technique grew, I decided to follow my passion and improve my training academically. Currently such is my passion for this world, which has led me to dedicate myself to it professionally.
