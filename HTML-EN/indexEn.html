about me

bringing your idea to life together

Paco Hidalgo, (Valencia, 1970)

Window dresser by profession and fallero artist, painter and illustrator of vocation and vital need. He studied Academic Drawing in some private academies in Valencia, but his training in general could be say that he is self-taught.

Children's fallas, sketches for other artists, collaborations as painter and decorator in different workshops, retardist, illustrator and graphic designer with some of Paco’s asthmatic facets. "It’s never too late to learn" This phrase that one day his maternal grandfather told him is the one he wears as flagship from rising until he lies down

tempera

A technique that uses a denser paint consistency achieved by emulsifying yolks and pigment and diluting everything with water. It is commonly applied with brush or paddle.
